How it works

Four steps. No middleman.

01

Generate QR

Create a standard UPI intent QR code with amount and your VPA. Works with any bank.

→
02

Customer pays

They scan with GPay, PhonePe, Paytm — any UPI app. Money goes directly to your bank.

→
03

Connect sources

Link your Gmail. Bank sends a payment confirmation email. upiagent reads it automatically.

→
04

AI verifies

LLM parses the email, matches amount + time + dedup. 4-layer security. Payment confirmed.
